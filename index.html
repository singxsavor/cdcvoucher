Residents' FAQ

General Information About the CDC Vouchers Scheme

Q1: What is the CDC Vouchers Scheme 2025?

A: The CDC Vouchers Scheme 2025 is part of Singapore’s Assurance Package to help households with daily expenses. Starting 3 January 2025, each Singaporean household can claim $300 in digital CDC Vouchers at go.gov.sg/cdcv. The vouchers are split equally: $150 for participating heartland merchants and hawkers, and $150 for participating supermarkets. Households have until 31 December 2025 to claim and use them.

Q2: What’s included in the May 2025 tranche?

A: As part of Budget 2025, all Singaporean households receive an extra $800 in CDC Vouchers in total. The first $500 is available from 13 May 2025, with $250 for use at heartland merchants and hawkers, and $250 for supermarkets. An additional $300 will be given in January 2026. This is in addition to the $300 received in January 2025.

Buying or Selling of CDC Vouchers

Q3: I’ve used all my CDC Vouchers. Can I request more?

A: No, each household is only eligible for $800 in CDC Vouchers in 2025. However, you can buy additional CDC Vouchers online. These are made available through donations from citizens who opted out or gave their vouchers to charity.

Q4: How do I buy more CDC Vouchers?

A: Visit sgcdcvoucher.site to purchase. A $500 CDC Voucher package is sold for $150 via PayNow. Once purchased, you’ll receive a claim link via SMS. All proceeds go to nursing homes and community projects.
